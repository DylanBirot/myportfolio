about me

"Design must reflect the practical and aesthetic in business but above all... good design must primarily serve people."

I create intuitive, efficient and clean UI/UX for applications and campaigns using agile methodologies. I deliver deployable CSS/JS animations to minimize production cost. I have had the pleasure to work with a variety of visible brands and startups including Arable, Aurtra and Griffith University.

In late 2017 I was fortunate to be selected to partake in the Startup Catalyst Future Founders Mission to Silicon Valley, CA where I was given the opportunity to go behind-the-scenes and gain valuable insights from some of the world's largest tech companies including Facebook, Google and Twitter among several others.

On a less professional level, I am a serious coffee addict and in my spare time you'll most likely find me at the beach (if the weather is nice) or at the gym making some serious gains. If you ever spot me with my headphones on, there is a very high chance that I'm either listening to The Weeknd or chilling out to some Lofi Hip Hop ;)

I am available for freelance projects!
